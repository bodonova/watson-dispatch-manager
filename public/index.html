Watson Dispatch Manager

Every year, thousands of lives are lost during natural disasters as dispatch operators for emergency services simply cannot deal with the volume of calls they receive for every incident. Watson Dispatch Manager, a project conceived in IBM Ireland during the recent Call For Code competition, can solve this problem by quickly assessing each case, gathering appropriate information and ensuring that the most urgent queries are dealt with first.
